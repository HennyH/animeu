About us



What we do

With the rise of anime culture in recent years we recognise a greater need for connecting the so called weebs around the world. As such, Animeu was born, created as a tool for weebs to express themselves and the love they have for their waifus.

Our mission is to provide a platform for every waifu to receive their love, and for our users to proudly declare their irrefutable truth: Who is Best girl. The platform also helps weebs find their next anime to put on their watch list using a revolutionary babe-first search approach.

How to use our website

Top of the screen resides several tabs with different functionality for your enjoyment.

Battle Tab

The battle tab allows you to engage in polls that will results in waifus accross the range to grow or decrease in popularity.

Feed

Feed shows the performance of different waifus, whether it be on leaderboard or in recent battles.

Profile

A place where you can rate your own waifus that you see fit, and it will also diplay your favourite waifus recent battles.
